Register for pre-orientation

Date: from 29 MAY to 30 MAY

Welcome!

Newcastle is for first-year and new transfer students to learn more about university life. We specially prepared pre-orientation activities.

How To register for pre-orientation

Go To The: https://www.newcastle.edu.au/study/new-students/orientation

Pre-orientation has what

1.School exhibition

The students are unfamiliar with many places when they just came to the school, so the school exhibition is very important. In this activity, Participating students follow the teacher’s guide to learn about the history of the school and how the school was built to give students a better understanding of the school’s history. Get to know the school

2. Icebreaker

We have a lot of fun icebreaker games in store, I can’t go into details about them, but I promise they will be great fun. The purpose is to make friends who have just arrived on campus, and international students can communicate with each other through games.

3. Professional Determination Seminar

In this session, we will invite outstanding teachers from each of our majors to introduce the content of each major and the possibilities after graduation. related work. The purpose is to give direction to students who have just entered the university without clear goals.

4. Community talent show

University life is not only about professional study, but also the activities of clubs are very important. In this event, various societies will perform interesting’s programs. The purpose is to stimulate students’ desire to understand the community

Commonly questions

Can I choose to participate in activities?

Yes, Newcastle University respects your personal will very much. You can choose the activities you want to participate in

Can I ask for the contact information of the seniors during the talent activity?

Yes, we do not restrict any communication with students
